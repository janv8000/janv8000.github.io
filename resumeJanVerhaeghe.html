Projects

Recall

Client: APB – Association Pharmaceutique Belgique – Algemene Pharmaceutische Bond : Analysis and development march 2014 - … One of the services the APB provides to its members is handling the logistic and financial aspects of large-scale product recalls, both for medicines and other health products.

The newly developed 'Recall' application streamlined this entire process. Error-prone paper forms were replaced by an electronic registration application and each shipment was accompanied by a barcode, giving an end-to-end visibility into the status and whereabouts of a shipment. The APB receiving department was equipped with touch-enabled screens and an intuitive tool to verify package contents.

Release of this application is planned Q1 2015.
